## Decisions Made
- **L2 scaling = `sum_all_weights`** (per-object docCount, ==12 here), not `sum_eff_weights` (pairwise-weight total). The instrumented log shows the Newton denom is `-SumDer2 + l2*(sumAllWeights/docCount)` with `sumAllWeights==docCount==12`. Verified: `sum_eff_weights` re-diverges PairLogit at Splits index 6.
- **Root cause re-diagnosed:** the prior "~6x / ~23-denominator" theory was wrong. Raw per-leaf deltas were correct (leaf3 = 0.5/3.25 = 0.1538); the missing piece was `NormalizeLeafValues` document-weighted centering. Confirmed: per-leaf raw deltas minus the document-weighted mean reproduce model.json leaf values to <=1e-9.
- **`leaf_weights` are document weights**, not pairwise weights (upstream `SumLeafWeights(GetWeights(TargetData))`, `train.cpp:456,609`); model.json stores `[8,3,0,1]`.
- **PairLogitPairwise escalated, not weakened:** stays `#[ignore]`'d with the precise divergence recorded; no tolerance change, no fabricated fixture.
