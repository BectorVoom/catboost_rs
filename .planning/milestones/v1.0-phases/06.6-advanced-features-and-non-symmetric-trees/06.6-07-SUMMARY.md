## Accomplishments
- **`shap_interaction_values(model, cols, n_features)`** in `shap.rs` (`shap_interaction_values.cpp`): per object the symmetric `(n_feat+1)²` matrix. Added FixedFeatureParams (On/Off) to both SHAP recursions (`TConditionsFeatureFraction` + `ExtendFeaturePathIfFeatureNotFixed`), then `Φ(i,j)=(contribOn_j−contribOff_j)/2` for `i≠j` and `Φ(i,i)=ϕ(i)−Σ_{j≠i}Φ(i,j)`; the bias slot (last index) gets the model bias (`SetBiasValues`). Oracle-locked ≤1e-5 (oblivious AND non-symmetric).
- **`prediction_diff(model, cols, n_features)`** (`compare_documents.cpp:12-249`): NOT a SHAP quantity — the indicator-coefficient algorithm. Per tree, flip each depth bit and attribute the leaf-value diff to `floatFeatureImpact[feat][borderSlot]`; per feature take the max cumulative change toward the other doc's prediction (`GetPredictionDiffSingle`); result `=|impact[0][f]+impact[1][f]|`. Oracle-locked ≤1e-5 on `X[:2]`.
- **`sage_values(model, cols, n_features)`** — deterministic structural surrogate (mean `|SHAP|` per feature) per **D-6.6-11 fallback (b)**; structural oracle (shape, finiteness, determinism, active-feature-support match vs the captured upstream vector). The `≤1e-5` seed-match value oracle is deferred (needs the upstream `TRestorableFastRng64(228)`+Shuffle+imputer+metric subsystem).
- **Non-symmetric TreeSHAP** (D-6.6-10): `calc_subtree_weights` generalized to the node-graph single-row fold (`reversedTree`); `shap_recurse_non_symmetric` walks `step_nodes` children (NOT bit-indexing) with `node_id_to_leaf_id`-attached leaves; `map_node_id_to_is_go_right` picks the per-object go/skip side. Oblivious arm byte-identical (D-6.6-05). Non-symmetric ShapValues + local-accuracy (`Σshap+bias==predict_raw`) oracle-locked ≤1e-5.
- **Oracle fixtures** (offline, catboost 1.2.10 `.venv`, seed-pinned, `thread_count=1`): oblivious + non-symmetric ShapInteractionValues, PredictionDiff, non-symmetric ShapValues, SAGE (captured), plus `gen_fixtures.py` (which also reverse-maps the bias-slot index for Open Question 2).
