Purpose: same de-risking rationale as 15-01 — exercise the divergent helper/tie-break DIRECTLY (no e2e device-grow) so a future device-grow wiring inherits a trustworthy oracle.
Output: an `n==0` guard in `query_helper.rs`; a tolerant deterministic tie-break in `pairwise.rs` (extracted to a `pub(crate)` selector so the oracle can unit-test it); two new oracles.
</objective>
