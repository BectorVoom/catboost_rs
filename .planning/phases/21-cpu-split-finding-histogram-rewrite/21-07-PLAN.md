<objective>
Close as much of the residual PERF-01 constant-factor as is PARITY-SAFELY recoverable.
21-06 shipped a genuine O(n_bins²)→O(n_bins) scan, a flat scatter-add build, and a
retained-parent subtraction advance — all VERIFIED — but the roadmap's literal "flat
within noise 32→254" bar was re-measured at ~3.3× (target ≤2.5×) and remains open.

ROOT CAUSE (already investigated + MEASURED 2026-07-06 via the CB_PERF sweep; do NOT
re-derive): per_tree_ms decomposes as a flat floor ≈1.7ms + ~0.026 ms/bin (n=10000,
nf=20, depth=6). The n_bins-linear term IS the split-scoring pass —
`select_level_plain`/`select_level_perturbed`/`best_split_for_leaf` →
`scan_borders_to_leaf_stats` (histogram.rs) + `multi_dim_split_score` (score.rs). It is
O(n_bins·n_leaves·n_features) per level and n-INDEPENDENT (the histogram cell count does
not depend on row count), hence ALGORITHMICALLY IRREDUCIBLE. The flat floor is binning +
the scatter build (O(n·nf)). Flatness requires n≫n_bins·n_leaves — which FAILS at
n=10000/depth-6 (n_bins·n_leaves≈16K>10K) and would only hold at n≥100k. Official
CatBoost is itself ~2.1× at this size, not flat. **This plan does NOT chase flatness**;
the literal bar is proven unreachable at the harness size and is documented as such.

THE RECOVERABLE GAP (this plan's target): the allocation churn constant, ~3.3× → ~2.3×.
Two per-candidate allocation sources remain:
1. `multi_dim_split_score` allocates 2 short-lived heap `Vec<f64>` per candidate border
   (num_terms; and, for Cosine, den_terms), called n_bins·n_features× per level.
2. `scan_borders_to_leaf_stats` materializes a full `Vec<Vec<Vec<LeafStats>>>`
   (n_borders·dim·2·n_parent) that the caller then re-reads border-by-border.

THE FIX (parity-safe scan+score FUSION): fuse the border scan and the scoring into one
pass. Per border, compute the 2·n_parent per-dim LeafStats inline from the running prefix
(FALSE = running prefix, TRUE = total − prefix — the SAME values today's scan produces)
and score them via a REUSED scratch buffer (clear + push + `cb_core::sum_f64`), returning
per-border scores directly. CRITICAL PARITY CONSTRAINT: keep the EXACT current per-border
summation — a FRESH `sum_f64` over all 2·n_parent leaves in canonical leaf order per
border (dimension-then-leaf). This is BIT-IDENTICAL to today (no reorder); it only
eliminates the per-border Vec allocations and the giant materialization. Do NOT introduce
a running/incremental num/den ACROSS borders — that would reorder the fold and re-touch
the ≤1e-5 crux, and is EXPLICITLY FORBIDDEN.

Purpose: recover the constant-factor (PERF-01 partial) and reduce allocation churn
(PERF-03) WITHOUT regressing the bit-exact/≤1e-5 parity crux (PERF-02, honored as a
constraint, not a target). Success = allocation-constant recovery + parity preservation,
framed with the honest "flat is unreachable at this harness size" evidence.

Output: a scratch-reusing `multi_dim_split_score_into` in score.rs, a fused
`scan_and_score_borders` in histogram.rs, all three callers rewired, a bit-identity
equivalence test, an atomic zero-additional-flip oracle proof, a re-recorded CB_PERF
32→254 sweep, and a 21-07-SUMMARY documenting the constant-factor recovery + the honest
flatness-unreachable framing.
</objective>
