Wave 2: implement **MultiLogloss** and **MultiCrossEntropy** (LOSS-02 multilabel) on the N-dim foundation. Per upstream (`tensor_search_helpers.cpp:236-238`) both losses dispatch to the **same `TMultiCrossEntropyError`** class — a single per-dimension **diagonal** der path (`error_functions.h:781-820`), two enum names.
