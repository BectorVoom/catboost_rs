**As a** developer training a ranking model with CatBoost-rs on a GPU, **I want to** have the pairwise split score computed device-resident on rocm from the 4-channel pairwise histogram, **so that** GPU ranking training grows trees whose structure matches the CPU pairwise path.

After this plan: the pairwise scorer runs on rocm AND cross-oracles against `calculate_pairwise_score`, and a ranking/PairLogit fixture grows a device tree matching the CPU pairwise structure — the final vertical slice closing GPU-01.
