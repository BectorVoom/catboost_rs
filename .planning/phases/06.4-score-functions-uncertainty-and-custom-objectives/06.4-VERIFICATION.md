### D-6.4-06 Weakened-Oracle Caveat (Non-Negotiable)

The five LOSS-09 score functions (SolarL2, NewtonL2, NewtonCosine, LOOL2, SatL2) are **GPU-only** in catboost 1.2.10 — the CPU path rejects them at `oblivious_tree_options.cpp:146`. There is no upstream-CPU training ground truth. The oracle is **transcribe-then-self-oracle**: the Rust port reproduces the hand-computed CUDA `score_calcers.cuh` arithmetic on chosen leaf-stat inputs (≤1e-12), NOT a ≤1e-5-vs-upstream-CPU training lock.

This caveat is:
- Explicitly stated in `score_functions_test.rs` module doc-comment lines 4-11
- Documented in `06.4-CONTEXT.md` decision D-6.4-06
- Reflected in `EScoreFunction` variant doc-comments in `runtime.rs`
- Recorded in SUMMARY.md for Plan 06.4-01

The Newton live-search der2 threading into the CPU greedy-search path is **intentionally deferred to Phase-7 GPU** (these five functions are CPU-unreachable upstream; wiring der2 through the CPU tree-search would be untestable scaffolding). The `reduce_leaf_stats_newton` fill primitive and the 7-variant `split_score` dispatch are shipped and unit-tested.

**This caveat does NOT block phase passage.** It is a documented, user-confirmed scope decision, not a failed requirement.
