<objective>
Build the single largest kernel extension of the milestone: the partition-aware `pointwise_hist2` (`fullPass=false`) keyed by `leaf_of[obj]` into `2^level` slots, the histogram **subtraction trick** (parent − smaller-sibling, with the weight-channel `max(0)` clamp), and wire the LOCKED deterministic fixed-point `Atomic<u64>` accumulator into the histogram merge (GPUT-06 step 0). Prove the fill+subtraction matches the CPU leaf-keyed scatter to ≤1e-4 with a kernel-level self-oracle.

Purpose: This is the device capability that unlocks depth>1 oblivious trees (GPUT-05) and the memory-lean speed lever (D-04 smaller-sibling-direct, parent-resident) that approaches parity. The deterministic accumulator (GPUT-06) is intrinsic to this kernel — the existing depth-1 histogram's naked `fetch_add` (kernels.rs:712) is the non-deterministic source this phase must remove.
Output: two new `#[cube]` behaviors in kernels.rs, their host launch fns in mod.rs (with V5 buffer-size guards), and a passing kernel self-oracle in grow_loop.rs.
</objective>
