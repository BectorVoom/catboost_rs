<objective>
Multi-permutation fold machinery (ORD-01) — the foundation of CatBoost's anti-leakage core and the D-03 linchpin. Both our Rust impl and upstream are seeded by the bit-exact Phase-1 `TFastRng64` port, so this is reproducible to the exact index. Per D-05 this is the first ordering layer after one-hot, locked by a permutation-ONLY oracle so a divergence isolates to the permutation/fold math before any CTR or boosting value depends on it.

Purpose: Permutation reproduction is locked as its own stage BEFORE any value comparison (D-03). A prefix-boundary off-by-one in body/tail is exactly the bug the per-object oracle exists to catch — and it can only be trusted if the permutation underneath it is exact first.
Output: `permutation.rs` (Fisher-Yates over TFastRng64), `fold.rs` (TFold body/tail), BoostParams permutation knobs, and the permutation + fold-prefix oracles.
</objective>
