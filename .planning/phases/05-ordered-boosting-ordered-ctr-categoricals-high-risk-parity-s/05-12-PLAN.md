<objective>
Build the CTR-split SCORING + ctr_data BAKE + apply-path Scale/Shift half of the
ORD-05 training pipeline, and close the FULL multi-tree e2e oracle. Score the
materialized CTR-feature columns (Plan 05-11) as split candidates during oblivious
tree growth, bake each chosen CTR split's whole-set inference CtrValueTable into
Model.ctr_data with the correct Scale/Shift, thread Scale/Shift through the apply
path, and update the e2e oracle to drive training through `train_cat` — so
`tensor_ctr_e2e_oracle_predictions_match_upstream` passes ≤1e-5 vs upstream
catboost 1.2.10 unweakened.

Purpose: This is the algorithmic + apply-correctness completion of ORD-05. The
upstream fixture model (verified: 5 trees, 2 splits/tree, ALL OnlineCtr on cat
feature 0, Borders prior 0.5/1, Scale=15 Shift=0, borders [2.999, 7.999]) cannot
be reproduced until (a) the CTR feature is scored into the tree, (b) the chosen
splits' ctr_data is baked, and (c) the apply path applies the model's Scale/Shift
instead of the current hardcoded 1.0/0.0 — which would never reach the [2.999,
7.999] scaled-border space.

Output: CTR-feature scoring in tree.rs, ctr_data baking in boosting.rs,
Scale/Shift threaded into apply.rs, and a GREEN unweakened
`tensor_ctr_e2e_oracle_predictions_match_upstream`. Closes ORD-05 / Roadmap SC-5.
