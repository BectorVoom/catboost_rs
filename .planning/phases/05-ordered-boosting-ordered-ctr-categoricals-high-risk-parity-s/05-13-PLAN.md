<objective>
Build the algorithmic heart of the corrected ORD-05 fix: the TWO-MATERIALIZATION
CTR pipeline the research (05-CTR-LEAF-VALUE-RESEARCH.md) root-caused and
empirically reproduced bit-exact on all 5 trees. Score the IDENTITY-learning-fold
CTR-feature column (Plan 05-11) as split candidates during oblivious tree growth
(the tree STRUCTURE), AND materialize a SECOND CTR-feature column under the
AveragingFold's SHUFFLED permutation (Plan 05-12) to compute the per-object leaf
indices + per-leaf weights for LEAF-VALUE estimation — keeping the existing
Gradient leaf formula unchanged.

This corrects the WRONG load-bearing assumption in the original 05-12 (CONTEXT
D-06: "CTR splits change tree STRUCTURE not leaf math; leaf values stay
Plain-identical"). The research proved leaf VALUES are estimated on a DIFFERENT
permutation (the shuffled AveragingFold) than structure (the identity learning
fold): structure partition [6,0,9,15], leaf-value partition [6,0,7,17], apply
partition (whole-set totals) — three distinct CTR materializations. The leaf
FORMULA (`sumDer/(count + l2·sumW/N)·lr`) is already correct in
compute_leaf_deltas; what was missing is the second (averaging-fold) CTR
materialization driving leaf_of.

Purpose: This is the part the prior executor's wall was on — single-fold
partitions miss the model leaf values by ~5e-3 (500× over the gate). The
research showed the averaging-fold (shuffled) partition + the existing Gradient
formula reproduces ALL FIVE TREES bit-exact. This plan delivers exactly that.

Output: CTR-feature scoring in tree.rs (structure), a second averaging-fold CTR
materialization in train_cat, leaf_of/leaf_weights computed from it, and a
GrownTree carrying the chosen CTR splits — verified by a scoring/partition test.
The ctr_data bake + apply Scale/Shift + e2e hard gate are Plan 05-14.
</objective>
