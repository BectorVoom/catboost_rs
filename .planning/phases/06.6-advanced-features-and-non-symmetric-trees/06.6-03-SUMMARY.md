## Decisions Made
- **Bit-exact = our-serialization round-trip, not byte-identity vs upstream file.** The upstream non-symmetric `.cbm` is 8112/7608 bytes vs our 1576/1032 because upstream carries InfoMap/GUID/feature-metadata our encoder omits — identical situation to the pre-existing oblivious path (which asserts prediction/model round-trip, not upstream byte-identity). Verified our-save→load→identical model + re-save byte-stable for both scenarios.
- **`Model.non_symmetric_trees` field added; all construction sites updated** per the integration note (a model is all-oblivious XOR all-non-symmetric).
- **json nested-form leaf ordering differs from the cbm flat-form** (json pre-order DFS vs upstream's flat layout). Both are internally consistent; reconciling them into one apply-correct ordering is exactly the 06.6-04 (grower) / 06.6-05 (apply) work — surfaced as the harness's expected-fail at the LeafValues/StagedApprox stages.
