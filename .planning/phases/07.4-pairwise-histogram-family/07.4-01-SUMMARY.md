## Decisions Made
- **Compare->histId mapping (the medium-confidence item, A6):** distilled the upstream warp-tile-distributed `AddPair` (flag passes, RotateRight, tiled_partition<16>) down to its accumulation semantics: per pair `(b1,b2,w)` and feature, `ge=(b1>=b2)`, `gt=(b1>b2)`, then `bin b1 -> histId {2*ge, 2*gt}` and `bin b2 -> histId {2*ge+1, 2*gt+1}`, each += w. Validated bit-exact by the self-oracle (host reference and kernel share the identical formula). Derived from the merge `weightLeq->{0,1}`, `weightGe->{2,3}`, `isSecondBin` = low bit (lines 240-256 of `pairwise_hist_one_byte_5bit.cuh`).
- **pairs encoding:** two parallel u32 arrays (D-7.4-03 discretion), FROZEN.
- **n_objects as a runtime kernel scalar:** so the cindex stride is over OBJECTS not pairs (Pitfall 3 — the #1 stride bug, avoided by passing n_objects explicitly).
