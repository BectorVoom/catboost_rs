<objective>
Grow a depth-1 oblivious tree fully on device with the Cosine GPU-default score, over a residency wrapper that uploads the quantized matrix once and keeps approx/der1 device-resident across iterations. This is the heaviest plan — but ~80% exists and is Phase-7-oracle-validated (grow_oblivious_tree_into, the der seam, Cosine in score_split, calc_average). The genuinely new work: the GpuTrainSession residency wrapper (GPUT-02), the apply_leaf_delta device kernel + no-read-back refactor (GPUT-03), wiring Cosine as the depth-1 default (GPUT-08), and the GpuBackend seam impls with the coverage gate (GPUT-04).

Purpose: This makes the device grow loop reachable and residency-correct — the >20x gap is the unwired host-light loop.
Output: session.rs residency wrapper; mod.rs apply_leaf_delta + handle-taking grow + Cosine default; gpu_backend.rs seam impls.

</objective>
